How scoring works
Longer reactions score more because points increase with seconds. Stronger reactions score more because weak = x1.0, medium = x1.5, strong = x2.2.
Laugh points = seconds × strength × 10, plus 3 pts for each separate laugh. Applause points = seconds × strength × 6, plus 2 pts for each separate applause. Longer moments also get a small extra impact bonus.
Laugh example
2 sec weak laugh ≈ 20 pts
2 sec strong laugh ≈ 44 pts
5 sec strong laugh ≈ 117 pts
Applause example
2 sec weak applause ≈ 12 pts
2 sec strong applause ≈ 26 pts
5 sec strong applause ≈ 72 pts
Bonus points: early first laugh gets rewarded, and the final applause gets an extra ending bonus.
First laugh bonus = 30 minus the first laugh time in seconds. Example: first laugh at 9.5 sec = 20.5 bonus pts. Final applause bonus = last applause seconds × strength × 8.
